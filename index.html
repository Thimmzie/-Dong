FAQ's

What is Đ ($DONG)?

A meme token on the polygon network that has no utility or value yet, just as Bitcoin, Doge, Shiba Inu and Pepe in their early stages. Just buy for fun to hold in your crypto wallet.

Can I lose money?

Yes, that is possible. But please don't invest/buy more than you can afford to lose.

How am I secured?

The only security you have is that you control the $DONG you receive in your own wallet, and it is recorded on the polygon blockchain network. You can also transfer $DONG to anyone you want, but there is no other security or real world value yet.

Can I make money?

It's possilbe if you hold, just as Bitcoin, Doge, Shiba Inu and Pepe, but currently there is no value or utility. $DONG is still just a meme coin.

How do I buy $Dong?

You can click Buy Now to buy with your debit/credit card via paypal; or click Connect Wallet (Metamask) to buy using crypto (Polygon).

Either way, you’ll receive $Dong directly into your wallet (instantly if bought with crypto via Connect Wallet, and within 24 hours if you buy $Dong with debit/credit card).
